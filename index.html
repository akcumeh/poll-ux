A note on this platform

Pollux does not predict election outcomes. It is an opinion tracker — a pulse check on public sentiment from users who choose to participate, which may not represent the broader Nigerian electorate.

Politician information is sourced from publicly available records. Pollux is independent and non-partisan with no affiliation to INEC or any political party.
